Our Happy Story

The Cornfield Cacti story begins in the sleepy town of Ames, Iowa. Or Johnston, Iowa, depending on who you ask. Pradyumn, Will, Cole, and Cameron met in their youth (since passed) through Johnston public schools. The four all ended up at Iowa State for their education. They formed the zygote of Cornfield Cacti soon after. Sam met Cameron and Pradyumn through their mutual employment at the school's IT call center. The boys (barring Cole) met up at Will's for their first practice. Cole came in some months later to bless us with low end.
